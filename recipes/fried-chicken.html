Crispy Fried Chicken


Description

This is a quick and easy recipe for making crispy fried chicken that is crunchy on the outside and juicy on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken pieces
  • 1 cup All-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Chicken powder or Garlic powder
  • Vegetable oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with salt and chicken powder for about 15 minutes.
  2. Coat each chicken piece thoroughly with all-purpose flour.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan and deep fry the chicken until it turns golden brown and crispy.
