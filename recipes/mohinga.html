Mohinga

Description

Mohinga is a rice noodle and fish soup from Myanmar and an essential part of Burmese cuisine, considered by many to be the national dish of Myanmar.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Fresh catfish or sea fish
  • Lemongrass, ginger, and garlic
  • Banana stem and toasted rice powder
  • Fish sauce and boiled eggs

Steps

  1. Boil the fish with lemongrass, ginger, and garlic, then separate the fish meat from the bones.
  2. Prepare the broth by mixing toasted rice powder and chickpea flour with water, then add onion and banana stem.
  3. Add the fish meat into the broth and let it simmer until cooked and aromatic.
  4. Serve by pouring the hot fish soup over rice noodles and garnish with boiled egg, cilantro, and crispy fritters.
